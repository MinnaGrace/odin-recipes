Creamy Chicken Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Slice the chicken in half lengthwise to create 2-4 thinner slices. Tenderize with a meat mallet.Season each side and dredge it in flour. Sear in olive oil and set aside. Deglaze the skillet with white wine and reduce by half.
  2. Add butter and garlic. Add flour and stir continuously. Add half and half, chicken broth, and seasonings in small splashes.
  3. Bring to a gentle bubble, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially while you boil the pasta to al dente.
  4. Stir in the cheese over low heat, then add the pasta, followed by the chicken. Stir to combine. Add a splash of pasta water to smooth it out if it's thicker than preferred. Serve once desired consistency is reached.
