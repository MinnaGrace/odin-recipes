Fried Chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Eggs
  • Plain flour
  • Cornflour
  • Oil
  • Herbs and Spices - salt, ginger, oregano, sage, garlic, thyme, onion powder, paprika, black pepper, white pepper, chili powder.

Steps

  1. Mix the butter milk and egg togther
  2. Season the chicken with paprika, saly and pepper
  3. Pour the buttermilk micture over the chicken and coat well.
  4. Refrigerate the chicken overnight
  5. Stir flour, herbs and spices in a bowl
  6. Coat each piece of chicken with the bowl from step 3
  7. Fry the chicken until it's golden brown
  8. Let the chicken cool, and then enjoy.
